Bookings

Seating: Walk-ins only

We are currently not taking reservations, and are operating as walk in only. During busy times you may have to queue for seating, and our staff will be managing a waitlist. If you would rather not queue, we also offer take away service.


Need your own space? We can host private functions

If your looking for space for that special celebration, a business function or to use as a filming backdrop get in touch. Eggztra cafe has a private function room avaliable to book, and we are also rent out the cafe for larger events.

For private bookings please call us on 01156 772934 or emil us at eggztra@gmail.com with details of your event.
